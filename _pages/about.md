Hossein is a computational designer with a strong interest in machine learning and deep learning, particularly in their application to sustainable architecture. He holds a master's degree in architecture from [Tarbiat Modares University](https://en.modares.ac.ir/) in Tehran. His thesis was developed under the supervision of [Dr. Mansour Yeganeh](https://www.modares.ac.ir/en-pro/academic_staff/yeganeh) from Tarbiat Modares University and [Dr. Damon Bolhassani](https://ssa.ccny.cuny.edu/blog/people/damon-bolhassani/) from the City College of New York.
{:.justified-text}
Since 2019, he has been sharing his passion for architecture and technology as a registered teacher with the Iran Ministry of Education. He is currently focused on advancing his expertise in machine learning and deep learning, with a growing interest in research-driven approaches and the application of AI to explore more sustainable architectural solutions.
